Questions? We've got answers.

Do I have to boost all my properties?▾
Not at all. You choose which properties to boost and at what level. Apply different tiers to different listings based on each property's needs.
Can I change or cancel anytime?▾
Yes. Upgrade, downgrade, or cancel your boost level whenever you want. There are no long-term contracts or cancellation fees.
How quickly will I see results?▾
Your boosted placement takes effect immediately. Most hosts notice increased impressions within the first week.
Will boosting guarantee more bookings?▾
Boosted listings drive more visibility to your properties. More travelers will see them. What happens next depends on you: competitive pricing, compelling photos, and clear descriptions turn that attention into bookings.
How is exposure calculated?▾
Exposure measures how often your listing appears in traveler search results compared to Standard-level listings in the same market over the same time period.
